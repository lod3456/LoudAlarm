Privacy Policy

Solve To Snooze does not collect, store, or share personal data.

Data Collection

This app stores alarm settings locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Notifications

The app uses local notifications to trigger alarms. Notification permissions are required for alarms to function.

Analytics & Tracking

Solve To Snooze does not use analytics, tracking, or third-party advertising.

Data Security

All data remains on your device and is never shared.
